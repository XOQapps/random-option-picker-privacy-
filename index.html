Privacy Policy for Random Option Picker

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Random Option Picker respects your privacy.

Data collection

Random Option Picker does not create user accounts and does not intentionally collect, store, or share personal information on external servers.

Local storage on your device

The app may store the following information locally on your device to support app features:

  • option lists created by you
  • picker title
  • language preference
  • theme and appearance settings
  • other app preferences you choose to save

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Data sharing

Random Option Picker does not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to third parties.

Data retention and deletion

Data stored by the app remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. If you disable saving between sessions, saved option data is removed according to the app’s behavior.

Children’s privacy

Random Option Picker is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
